Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in a template purchase?

When you purchase a template, you receive all the necessary files to use and customize the template, including source files, documentation, and any assets used in the demo version. You also get access to future updates and our customer support.

Do I need technical skills to use your templates?

Most of our templates are designed to be user-friendly and require minimal technical knowledge. Website templates may require basic HTML/CSS knowledge for customization, while presentation templates can be edited using standard software like PowerPoint or Keynote. We provide detailed documentation to guide you through the process.

Can I use the templates for multiple projects?

Our standard license allows you to use a template for a single end product (website, presentation, etc.). If you need to use the template for multiple projects, you'll need to purchase additional licenses or contact us about our extended license options.

Do you offer customization services?

Yes, we offer customization services for our templates. If you need help adapting a template to your specific requirements, our team of designers and developers can assist you. Contact us for a quote based on your needs.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with your purchase. However, refunds are not available after you've downloaded the template files or if the template has been customized for your specific needs.
