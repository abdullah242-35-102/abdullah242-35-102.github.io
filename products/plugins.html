Frequently Asked Questions

Are your plugins compatible with the latest platform versions?

Yes, we regularly update our plugins to ensure compatibility with the latest versions of WordPress, Shopify, and other platforms. We typically release updates within days of major platform updates to ensure seamless functionality.

Do I need technical skills to install and use your plugins?

Most of our plugins are designed to be user-friendly and require minimal technical knowledge. Installation is typically a simple process, and we provide detailed documentation to guide you through setup and configuration. For more complex customizations, basic knowledge of the platform may be helpful.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer comprehensive support for all our plugins, including installation assistance, configuration help, and troubleshooting. Our standard support package includes email support with a 24-48 hour response time. Premium support options with faster response times and direct access to our development team are also available.

Can I use your plugins on multiple websites?

Our standard license allows you to use a plugin on a single website. If you need to use the plugin on multiple sites, we offer multi-site licenses at discounted rates. Please contact our sales team for more information about multi-site licensing options.

Do you offer refunds if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all our plugins. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, simply contact our support team within 14 days of purchase for a full refund. Please note that refunds are not available for customization services or after the 14-day period.
